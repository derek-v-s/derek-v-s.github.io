The Fundamental Virtues

I define a virtue as a cognitive process that leads to the support of well-being. I'm working on creating a list of fundamental or foundational virtues that all other virtues are forms or composites of.

Composure
consciously shaping or preventing reactions
Considerateness
wanting to support well-being and remaining vigilant to that end
Humility
recognizing one's own limitations
Introspectiveness
contemplating one's own thoughts and behavior
Prioritization
balancing and sequencing energy use to meet needs
Tact
considering optimal ways to meet needs
