The Heart of Virtue

I define a virtue as a cognitive process that leads to the support of well-being. I call the minimal set of virtues needed to eliminate crime and conflict the governing virtues or the heart of virtue.

Introspectiveness
contemplation of one's own actions and cognitive processes
Other-care
desire and vigilance to support the well-being of others
Prioritization
ascertainment of what's important and the optimal use of energy
Self-care
desire and vigilance to support your own well-being
