The Heart of Virtue

A virtue is a cognitive process that leads to the support of well-being. Helpfulness is the physical product of virtue, and equanimity is the psychological product of virtue.

Considerateness
desire to support well-being
Carefulness
vigilance to support well-being
Courage
willingness to risk one's own well-being in support of another's
Prioritization
ascertainment of the optimal use of energy
Introspectiveness
contemplation of one's own actions and cognitive processes
