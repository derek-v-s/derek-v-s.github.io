The Heart of Virtue

I define a virtue as a cognitive process that leads to the support of well-being. I call the minimal set of virtues needed to eliminate crime and conflict the governing virtues or the heart of virtue.

Benevolence
wanting to support well-being and remaining vigilant to that end
Composure
consciously shaping or preventing reactions
Introspectiveness
observing internal processes
Prioritization
balancing and sequencing energy use to meet needs
Skepticism
forming questions and theories rather than beliefs
Tact
considering optimal ways to meet needs
